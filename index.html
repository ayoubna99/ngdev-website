NGMed — Medical E-Commerce Platform

Problem

Managing product listings, orders, and vendor stock across multiple suppliers was being done manually in spreadsheets, leading to errors and slow fulfilment.

Solution

Mobile App

Guess My Grade — Student Performance App

Problem

University students had no reliable way to track module grades, estimate final averages, or plan which subjects to focus on — especially under the complex LMD grading system.

Solution

We built a cross-platform mobile app with grade entry, GPA calculation, and academic planning — published on both Android and iOS.

Result

Adopted by students across multiple universities. Consistent 4+ star rating with strong organic word-of-mouth growth among LMD and engineering cohorts.

Flutter Firebase Laravel
